Process

From your current site to cleaner HTML

1
You share your current site
Point WebSpark at your existing pages or paste in the HTML you already have.
2
We rebuild the layout
WebSpark restructures your content into cleaner sections, better typography, and a stronger visual hierarchy.
3
You keep full control
You get back pure HTML/CSS – no lock-in, no surprise scripts, and no dependency on a visual builder.

WebSpark is about clarity, not complexity – the output is meant to be readable by both browsers and humans.
